To use these interactive examples, start the Simulator or Connect a real device using the dashboard at the bottom of your screen.

This interactive tool allows you to safely generate a Master Key and initialize a Boks device.

CRITICAL WARNING

This operation is irreversible without the Master Key. The Master Key generated here is stored in your browser's LocalStorage. If you clear your browser data or lose this key, you will permanently lose administrative access to your device. Always back up your key immediately after generation.
